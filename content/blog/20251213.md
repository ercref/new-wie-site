      inclusion but noting a significant minority opinion preferring
      delay to the next fork.

      EIP-7688, EIP-8061, and EIP-8080.

## Research

- <a
  href="https://ethresear.ch/t/achieving-10gigagas-s-evm-execution-with-bal-and-parallel-execution/23632"
  target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">Achieving 10GigaGas/s
  EVM Execution</a> - A new research proposal outlines how Block-level
  Access Lists (BAL) combined with parallel execution could potentially
  achieve 10GigaGas/s EVM execution, dramatically scaling Ethereum's
  throughput.

- <a href="https://ethresear.ch/t/trustless-payments/23635"
  target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">Trustless Payments
  Research</a> - New research explores trustless payment mechanisms that
  could enhance security and reduce MEV extraction, with potential
  applications in the upcoming Glamsterdam upgrade.
