## Fusaka upgrade

- The <a
  href="https://blog.ethereum.org/2025/11/06/fusaka-mainnet-announcement"
  target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">Ethereum Foundation
  published the official Fusaka mainnet announcement</a>, confirming the
  upgrade will activate at slot 13,164,544 (December 3, 2025, 21:49:11
  UTC).

- <a
  href="https://blog.ethereum.org/2025/11/06/fusaka-mainnet-announcement"
  target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">Fusaka introduces
  Blob Parameter Only (BPO) forks</a> to safely scale blob throughput
  after PeerDAS activation. BPO1 will increase the per-block blob target
  to 10 and maximum to 15 on December 9, while BPO2 will further
  increase the target to 14 and maximum to 21 on January 7, 2026.

- The upgrade includes several key EIPs, including
  <a href="https://eips.ethereum.org/EIPS/eip-7594" target="_blank"
  rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">EIP-7594: PeerDAS</a>,
  <a href="https://eips.ethereum.org/EIPS/eip-7883" target="_blank"
  rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">EIP-7883: ModExp Gas Cost
  Increase</a>, and
  <a href="https://eips.ethereum.org/EIPS/eip-7951" target="_blank"
  rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">EIP-7951: Precompile for secp256r1
  Curve Support</a>.

## Client Releases
