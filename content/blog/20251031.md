## Highlight of the Week

- <a href="https://notes.ethereum.org/@bbusa/fusaka-bpo-timeline"
  target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">The Ethereum
  community has finalized the <strong>Fusaka mainnet upgrade date for
  December 3, 2025</strong>,</a> with BPO1 scheduled for December 9 and
  BPO2 for January 7, 2026. This major upgrade includes PeerDAS for
  enhanced data availability and will significantly improve Layer 2
  scalability. Client releases are expected on November 3, one month
  before the mainnet upgrade.
