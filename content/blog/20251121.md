## Notable at app layer

- Stream Finance experienced a
  <a href="https://rekt.news/loop-contagion/" target="_blank"
  rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">catastrophic failure</a> on
  November 18, with \$93 million vanishing and their stablecoin xUSD
  crashing 77%. The collapse followed October warnings about Stream's 4x
  leverage and recursive loops, resulting in \$285 million in exposure
  being unwound. While Trevee (a related protocol) recovered through
  aggressive action, most depositors face near-total losses.
